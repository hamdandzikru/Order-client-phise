Why Facebook?
Facebook is defined by our unique culture – one that rewards impact. We encourage our people to be bold and solve the problems they care most about. Facebook employees work in small teams that move fast and iterate to develop new products. Together, we help others build stronger communities—and we're just getting started.

We're Just Getting Started
Bringing the World Closer Together
So far, we've connected more than 3 billion people—which means we still have more than 4 billion to go. The scale and complexity of our work are unprecedented, and the work we do makes a real difference in the lives of people around the world. Over time, we’ve expanded the Facebook family to include Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, Augmented & Virtual Reality, and Facebook Connectivity—but when it comes to bringing the world closer together, we’re just getting started.

Connectivity

Through Facebook Connectivity, we’re working to bring access to the internet and all it has to offer to everyone on the planet—giving more people a voice, strengthening community bonds and creating economic opportunities.
Learn More

Artificial Intelligence

We’re using AI to help people get the information that’s most relevant to them in the most natural way—for example, translating languages automatically and automatically captioning photos to help visually impaired people better picture the world around them.
Learn More

Augmented & Virtual Reality

We're using AR and VR to build powerful new ways for people to experience the world, express themselves and connect with others. Our commitment to innovation ensures that users will continue to enjoy state of the art AR effects and VR experiences.
